Newo AI Receptionist for Odoo

Answers calls, books appointments, captures leads — 24/7.

Newo is a multilingual AI receptionist that handles inbound calls, SMS, WhatsApp and Telegram conversations on behalf of your business. This free connector links Newo with your Odoo CRM and Calendar.

What gets synchronised

  • Bookings — created as calendar.event records linked to the right resource.
  • Leads — created as crm.lead with the full transcript and recording URL attached.
  • Contacts — deduplicated against res.partner by phone and email.
  • Working hours — read from resource.calendar so the agent never books outside availability.

Subscription required

Installing and configuring this module is free. Running conversations requires an active Newo subscription. Sign up at newo.ai.
